Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

A traditional Kenyan staple meal that's nutritious and delicious. This combination of cornmeal porridge (ugali) with sautéed collard greens (sukuma wiki) is a favorite comfort food.

Ingredients

For Ugali:

  • 2 cups white cornmeal (maize flour)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

For Sukuma Wiki:

  • 2 bunches collard greens, chopped
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1-2 chili peppers (optional)

Instructions

For Ugali:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add salt to the water.
  3. Slowly add the cornmeal while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Reduce heat to low and continue stirring as the mixture thickens.
  5. Add the remaining water gradually as needed.
  6. Keep stirring until the ugali has a dough-like consistency.
  7. Cover and let it cook for about 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with sukuma wiki.

For Sukuma Wiki:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add chopped collard greens and stir well.
  5. Add coriander, salt, pepper, and chili peppers if using.
  6. Cover and cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve hot alongside ugali.
